Privacy Policy

Insightive LLC-FZ ("Insightive", "we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why and how you can exercise your rights under applicable law, including the UAE Personal Data Protection Law and the GDPR where relevant.

What we collect

We collect what you choose to send us: contact form submissions (name, email, company and message), career applications (your details and the CV you upload) and newsletter sign-ups. We also collect basic, aggregated analytics about how the site is used.

How we use it

To respond to your enquiry, assess job applications, send updates you asked for and improve the site. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for anything beyond the purpose it was given for.

Who we share it with

Only the service providers that help us run the site and our communications, such as our hosting, email and analytics providers, under terms that require them to protect it. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand site usage and the Meta pixel to measure advertising; both may set cookies and process limited data under their own privacy terms. You can accept or decline these via the consent banner on your first visit and change your choice anytime using the Cookies link in the footer. We do not sell your data or share it with anyone else without your consent.

How long we keep it

Only as long as the purpose needs or the law requires. Career submissions stay on file for future roles unless you ask us to remove them.

Your rights and contact

You can request access to, correction of or deletion of your data and you can object to its use. To exercise any of these, or for any question about this policy, write to legal@insightive.ltd. Insightive LLC-FZ, Meydan Free Zone, Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
